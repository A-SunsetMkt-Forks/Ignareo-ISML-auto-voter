Voting Policies and Procedures:

  1. A voter is any person who submits a form containing character selections in an ISML-hosted event. Voters are considered part of the ISML Community.
  2. Voting in any ISML-hosted match is completely voluntary. ISML shall not coerce any member of the Community to participate, and ISML shall not be held responsible for any person's actions as a result of voluntary participation.
  3. A vote may be cast only during the specified time period for that particular match and round. Once cast, a vote may be edited by its owner only during this time period and subject to approval by the Staff.
  4. Each voter is limited to one (1) vote per match per round. A voter may not vote on behalf of another person.
  5. IP addresses of voters will be logged, in order to determine authenticity and uniqueness. A voter may not circumvent the Policies stated in Article IV of the ISML Constitution by using multiple IP addresses to cast votes in the same round.
  6. Please campaign by encouraging new people to vote and old voters to not forget to vote. Campaign by posting materials that will make people love and vote for your favorite characters. Don't encourage cheating. If you are a forum moderator, a blog owner, or someone of position in any website, please discourage people from voting more than once in a single match.
  7. Voters may change their vote (if cast in error) by contacting the Staff by email at admin@internationalsaimoe.com. Vote change requests must include the IP address from which the vote was cast, the original incorrect vote, and the requested new vote.
  8. Votes involved in unethical activities or behaviors may be disregarded at the discretion of ISML staff.

Necklace/Pendant/Bracelet/Ring Match Instructions

  1. Rank the characters from 1 up to 8 in decreasing order of preference. Note that 1 signifies your top support and abstains signify no support. Thus, if you wish, you may vote for a single character by selecting “abstain” for the seven others. Or, you may choose to rank only the characters you like.
  2. Instead of simply choosing one contestant, with Instant Runoff Voting, you are allowed to rank all contestants in your order of preference. You can still vote for only one contestant or only rank the ones you like.
  3. Ballots are initially counted for each voter's top choice. If a contestant secures more than half of these votes, that contestant wins. Otherwise, the contestant in last place is eliminated and removed from consideration. Each vote for an eliminated candidate are now transferred to voter's next most preferred choice available. The top remaining choices on all the ballots are then counted again. This process repeats until one contestant is the top remaining choice of a majority of the voters. When the field is reduced to two, it has become an "instant runoff" that allows a comparison of the top two contestants head-to-head.

Wildcard Match Instructions

    Top 8 Advance

  1. For each Division, the 20 characters who finished between 7th to 11th in his or her group has been placed into a single poll. You are to indicate all the characters you like. Of the 20 characters in each poll, 8 will advance; the other 12 will be eliminated.

Summer Seasonal

    Top 16 Advance

  1. For Seasonal Tournament preliminary, please select the characters you like. You can select any number of characters. Top 16 in the arena will advance to the round of 16 for the seasonal tournament

Anime Idol Tournament THE iDOLM@STER Play-in Match

    Top 2 Advance

  1. Move the slider bar to a number that best reflects how much you like the character. 10.0 would be considered as you really love the character, while 0.1 would be considered as you having very little positive feelings about the character. Abstains will be considered as 0.0 score for the character. We will be using sum of all the scores from the voters.
